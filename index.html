About Painted Horizons

Painted Horizons LLC is a locally owned company focused on quality, reliability, and clear expectations. From the first walkthrough to the final touch-up, we treat your home like our own and stand behind our work.

We started with professional interior and exterior painting, and are growing into a “we do it all” partner for homeowners and small businesses. Under the Painted Horizons LLC umbrella, we offer painting, deck and fence staining, small prep repairs, basic digital visibility services, Amazon Hub–related support, and a box recycling option for the community.

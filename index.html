About Painted Horizons

Painted Horizons LLC is a locally owned painting company focused on quality, reliability, and clear expectations. From the first walkthrough to the final touch-up, we treat your home like our own and stand behind our work.

We specialize in professional interior and exterior painting, deck and fence staining, and the prep work that makes finishes last: caulking, patching, sanding, and surface repairs. If you want a clean, durable result with straightforward communication, you’re in the right place.
